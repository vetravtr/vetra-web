## The Vision

VETRA was born from a simple observation: most stablecoins lack true transparency. Users are expected to trust without verification. We set out to build a stablecoin where **trust is replaced by proof**.

### The Problem

- Centralized stablecoins operate as black boxes
- Reserves are often opaque or unaudited
- Retail and institutional users alike deserve better

### The Solution

VETRA is a **fully collateralized stablecoin** on Polygon, with every single VTR token backed 1:1 by USD-denominated reserves held at **FT Asset Management** in Sweden — a regulated financial institution with a 15-minute Proof-of-Reserves feed via Chainlink.

## Technical Architecture

VETRA runs on Polygon for low fees and fast settlement:

- **Token:** UUPS upgradeable, ERC-20 with AccessControl
- **Liquidity:** Concentrated on Sushi (V2 + V3)
- **Oracles:** Chainlink Price Feeds + Proof-of-Reserves
- **Reserves:** $100M in regulated custody
- **Fees:** 0% transfer tax

## What Makes VETRA Different

1. **Over-collateralized** — 333% ratio means every VTR has $3.33 in reserves
2. **Real-time verification** — anyone can verify reserves on-chain
3. **Institutional-grade** — Swedish-regulated custody
4. **Zero-fee transfers** — send VTR at Polygon's gas cost
